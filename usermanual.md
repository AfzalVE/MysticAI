# MysticAI - User Manual

Welcome to **MysticAI**, your premier platform for digital spiritual guidance, powered by artificial intelligence. This manual will guide you through the features and functionalities of the platform.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration & Login
To access the platform's features, you must create an account.
1. Navigate to the **Login** page.
2. If you don't have an account, click **Create Account** to go to the Registration page.
3. Fill in your Name, Email, and Password.
4. Once registered, log in to access your personalized spiritual journey.

---

## 2. The User Dashboard
Upon logging in, you will be directed to your **Dashboard**.
- **Profile Management**: View and edit your birth details (Date of Birth, Time of Birth, Place of Birth). These details are used to personalize your readings.
- **Change Password**: Securely update your account password.
- **Recent Readings**: View a history of your past Fortune and Tarot readings.
- **Upcoming Bookings**: Keep track of your scheduled live consultations.

---

## 3. Core Features

### ✨ Fortune Telling
Discover what the cosmos has in store for you.
- Your personal details (DOB, Time, Place) are automatically pre-filled from your profile. You may edit these if you wish to do a reading for someone else.
- Click **Generate Reading** to receive a personalized, AI-generated fortune and horoscope.
- All readings are automatically saved to your Dashboard.

### 🃏 Tarot Reading
Draw cards from a digital, interactive deck.
- Choose between different spread types (e.g., Single Card, Past/Present/Future).
- Select your cards from the mystical deck.
- The AI will interpret the specific combination of cards you drew and provide a deep, insightful reading.
- Your Tarot draws and interpretations are saved to your Dashboard.

### 🔮 AI Guide (Chat)
Engage in a continuous, spiritual conversation with our AI Mystic Guide.
- **Persistent Sessions**: Just like ChatGPT, your conversations are saved. Access your past sessions from the left sidebar.
- **New Consultation**: Start a brand new conversational thread anytime.
- The AI Guide remembers the context of your current session, allowing for deep, continuous spiritual exploration.

### 📅 Book Session
If you prefer a human touch, you can book a live consultation.
- Choose your consultation type (e.g., Live Tarot, Natal Chart Reading).
- Select a Date and Time.
- Provide any specific questions or areas of focus in the details box.
- Your booking will be saved and visible on your Dashboard.

---

## 4. Administrator Portal
MysticAI features a secure administration portal for managing the platform.

### Accessing the Admin Portal
- Navigate to `http://localhost:5173/admin-login` (or the equivalent production URL).
- Log in using your authorized Admin credentials. Regular user credentials will be rejected.
- You will be redirected to the **Admin Dashboard**.

### Admin Features
- **Dashboard Overview**: View high-level statistics including total users, total readings, chat sessions, and bookings.
- **Users**: View a complete list of registered users on the platform.
- **Readings**: Review all Fortune and Tarot readings generated on the platform, associated with the names of the users who requested them.
- **AI Chat Logs**: Monitor all AI chat sessions taking place on the platform.
- **Bookings**: Manage and review all upcoming user consultation bookings.
- **Profile**: Click "Profile" in the sidebar to seamlessly jump back to your regular user dashboard.
